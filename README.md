# Air pollution exposure in Generation Scotland: molecular fingerprints and health outcomes

Pre-print url: 

Individual scripts are in folders for each component of the study:
-	**01_Scotland_wide_pollution:** descriptive analysis of pollution levels across Scotland from 2005 – 2011 (incorporating the recruitment period of Generation Scotland)
  
-	**02_GS_pollution:** generating estimated pollutant exposures for GS participants and descriptive analysis of these exposures
  
-	**03_GS_Cox_models:** data preparation and models to assess association with incident disease including manifestations of cardiovascular disease and dementia
  
-	**04_GS_EpiAge:** data preparation for analysis of the association of epigenetic age acceleration with pollution exposure
  
-	**05_Multivariate_EWAS:** Scripts for the data preparation and running of Multivariate Bayesian EWAS
  
-	**06_Multivariate_PWAS:** Scripts for the data preparation and running of Multivariate Bayesian PWAS
  
-	**07_Univariate_EWAS:** Scripts for the data preparation and running of univariate Bayesian EWAS
  
-	**08_Univariate_EWAS**: Scripts for the data preparation and running of univariate Bayesian PWAS
  
-	**09_Summarise_Compare_EWAS_PWAS:** Scripts summarising results from the EWAS and PWAS models and comparing the results across methods
  
-	**10_Pollution_EpiScore:** Training and testing of pollutant episcores
